Welcome to Dreadlocks Salon

Who We Are?

We are a professional hair salon that specializes in dreadlocks and natural hair care. We offer a variety of services, such as dreadlocks installation, maintenance, styling, coloring, extensions, and more. Whether you want to start your dreadlocks journey, refresh your existing locks, or try something new, we have the perfect package for you. I believe that your hair is your best accessory, and I'm committed to helping you showcase your individuality. Whether you're looking for a sleek, modern cut, vibrant color, or a timeless classic style, I've got you covered. My goal is not just to style your hair but to enhance your confidence and beauty.

Available For Consultation on Hair related Issues.

I value open communication and actively listen to your desires, concerns, and ideas. Together, we'll create a look that complements your lifestyle and makes you feel fabulous. Our salon is located in Ashton-Under-Lyne, Greater Manchester, United Kingdom. We have a friendly and experienced team of stylists who are passionate about dreadlocks and natural hair. We use only high-quality products and tools to ensure the best results for your hair. We also provide free consultations and advice on how to take care of your dreadlocks at home.
