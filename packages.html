Packages

We offer a range of packages to suit your needs and budget. All our packages include a free consultation, shampoo, conditioner, and scalp massage. You can also add extra services, such as coloring, extensions, or styling, for an additional fee. Please note that the prices may vary depending on the length and thickness of your hair.

Package Description Price
Starter: This package is for those who want to start their dreadlocks from scratch. We use the backcombing method to create neat and tight dreadlocks. This package takes about 4 to 6 hours, depending on the size and number of dreadlocks you want. £150
Maintenance: This package is for those who already have dreadlocks and need to keep them healthy and tidy. We use the crochet hook method to tighten the roots and fix any loose hairs. This package takes about 2 to 3 hours, depending on the condition of your dreadlocks. £50
Styling: This package is for those who want to spice up their dreadlocks with some creative styling. We can do braids, buns, updos, mohawks, and more. This package takes about 1 to 2 hours, depending on the complexity of the style. £30
